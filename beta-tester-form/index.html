Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements for Orasis AI?

Orasis AI is a cloud-based platform accessible through modern web browsers. You'll need a stable internet connection and a computer with at least 8GB RAM for optimal performance.

How long does the alpha testing period last?

The alpha testing period is expected to run for 3-6 months, starting from February 2025. Beta testers will have continued access until the official launch.

Is there any cost involved in alpha testing?

No, alpha testing is completely free. Selected testers will receive full access to all features at no cost during the alpha period.

What kind of feedback are you looking for from alpha testers?

We are looking for feedback on usability, feature requests, performance, and any bugs or issues encountered. Your insights will help us refine the platform before the public launch.

What kind of support will alpha testers receive?

Alpha testers will have access to dedicated support channels, including direct email support, weekly office hours, and an exclusive Slack community for real-time assistance.

How is patient data protected during alpha testing?

We maintain HIPAA compliance and use end-to-end encryption for all data. Alpha testers must follow our data security protocols and can use anonymized test data provided by us.
